Course Overview

This lesson is for teachers who are interested in implementing digital game-based learning in their classroom. The purpose of this lesson is to design a learning activity that uses digital game-based strategies to deepen student engagement and learning in the classroom.

 

Lesson Objectives:

1. Evaluate the usage of digital game-based learning practices in a classroom.

2. Create a digital game-based activity for your educational environment.

3. Create a lesson plan for implementing any digital game-based learning approach for your classroom.
