About Me

I'm an education technology professional who thrives at the intersection of pedagogy and innovation. With a PhD in Curriculum & Instruction and 7+ years of experience spanning K-12, higher education, and nonprofit settings, I design scalable learning programs that are both research-grounded and learner-centered.

Currently, as Curriculum Manager at Code For Fun, I lead curriculum development for computer science programs reaching 35,000+ students across 300+ schools. I bring together instructional design, program management, and a passion for equity in education — from empowering women in Nepal through literacy programs to shaping AI literacy curricula in Silicon Valley classrooms.

I'm looking to bring my skills in curriculum strategy, cross-functional collaboration, and learning design to an education technology or learning & development role at a mission-driven tech company.
