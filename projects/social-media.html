Interactive Module

Social Media Guidelines & Expectations

An interactive module on responsible social media use, digital citizenship, and platform-specific guidelines for educational settings.

Overview

This SoftChalk module provides educators and students with comprehensive guidelines for responsible social media use in educational contexts. It covers digital citizenship principles, platform-specific best practices, privacy considerations, and strategies for using social media as a learning tool while maintaining professional boundaries.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand digital citizenship principles for social media use
  • Evaluate correct and incorrect social media practices in academic settings
  • Apply guidelines for professional social media presence
