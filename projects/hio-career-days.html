Curriculum Design

HIO Career Days for Girls

STEM career guidance workshops designed for high school girls in Nepal, featuring career day programming and mentorship frameworks.

Overview

As Curriculum Developer at Hands In Outreach (HIO), I designed and coordinated STEM career day workshops for high school girls in Nepal. These workshops introduced participants to STEM career pathways, connected them with women mentors in technology and science fields, and provided hands-on activities to spark interest in careers traditionally underrepresented by women in Nepal.

Workshop Components

Career Day Workshop (1-Day Saturday Program)

  • Introduction to STEM career pathways
  • Panel discussions with women STEM professionals
  • Hands-on activities and demonstrations
  • Goal-setting and action planning sessions

Career Workshop for High School Girls

  • Career exploration and self-assessment activities
  • Skills mapping and interest inventories
  • Resume and interview preparation
  • Networking and mentorship connections

STEM Career Guidance Session

  • Overview of STEM fields and opportunities
  • Breaking stereotypes in STEM education
  • Scholarship and resource information
  • Peer support group formation
