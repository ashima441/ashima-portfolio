Interactive Module

Self Care for College Students

An interactive module teaching evidence-based self-care strategies for physical, emotional, and social well-being.

Overview

This SoftChalk module was designed to help college students develop sustainable self-care practices. The module addresses the unique stressors facing college-aged learners and provides practical, evidence-based strategies across physical, emotional, and social domains. Interactive elements engage students in self-reflection and action planning.

Learning Objectives

  • Define self-care and recognize its importance for academic success
  • Identify physical, emotional, and social self-care strategies
  • Create a personalized self-care plan for the academic semester
