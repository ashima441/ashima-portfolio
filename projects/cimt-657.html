Curriculum Design

CIMT 657 Course Design

A complete graduate-level course design featuring an 8-module structure, detailed syllabus, course maps, and instructional resources for online delivery.

Overview

As a Graduate Teaching Assistant at Indiana State University, I designed the complete course structure for CIMT 657, a graduate-level course in the Curriculum, Instruction, and Media Technology department. This project encompassed full course design — from learning outcomes and module planning to assessment strategies and resource curation — following backward design principles.
